Healthy Nigerian Lifestyle Guide

Daily Walking

Aim for at least 30 minutes of brisk walking. It lowers blood pressure and improves cardio wellness.

Hydration Targets

Drinking 8 glasses of clean water keeps you hydrated, boosts metabolism, and flushes metabolic waste.

Fiber-rich Foods

Incorporate Nigerian complex carbs like yam, green plantains, and beans porridge to support blood sugar stability.

Quality Sleep

7 to 8 hours of deep night sleep lowers cortisol levels, helping control both heart rate and sugar parameters.
