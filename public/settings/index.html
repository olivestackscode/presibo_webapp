Frequently Asked Questions

How is the Unified Health Score calculated?

It rates health parameters from 1.0 to 10.0 based on daily logged steps, sleep duration, water intake, blood pressure bounds, and blood sugar readings.

What payment platform does the wallet use?

All transaction processes use the Paystack payment gateway supporting Naira (NGN) cards, bank transfers, and USSD codes.

Is Jitsi video connection encrypted?

Yes, all teleconsultation rooms utilize fully encrypted peer-to-peer audio and video transmission hosted by Jitsi Meet.
