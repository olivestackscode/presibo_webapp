Elderly Care Benefits

Remote Monitoring

24/7 health tracking for vital signs. Family and caregivers get alerts if attention is needed. Blood pressure, glucose, heart rate monitored continuously.

Medication Management

Never miss a dose. Automated reminders, pill organizers, and family notifications. Pharmacist review of all medications to prevent interactions.

Access to doctors specializing in elder care. Regular check-ups, chronic disease management, and age-appropriate screenings.

Resources and respite for family caregivers. Support groups, training, and professional backup when you need a break.

Fast access to emergency care. One-touch SOS button connects to medical team. Coordinated hospital admission when needed.
